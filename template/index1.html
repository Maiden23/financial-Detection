General Precautions

  • Use secure websites (look for "https" and a padlock icon).
  • Enable two-factor authentication for extra security.
  • Avoid public Wi-Fi for online transactions.
  • Monitor bank statements for any unauthorized activity.
  • Use strong, unique passwords and update them regularly.
  • Stay alert to phishing scams and avoid suspicious links.
  • Enable transaction alerts to track unusual activity.
